Symmetric NAT Detection

This tool helps you determine if your network is using Symmetric NAT.

Step 1: open the Chrome console, press Ctrl + Shift + J on Windows or Command + Option + J on macOS.

Step 2: click the "Test-1" button, wait for 1 minute, copy and save the console output.

Step 3: click the "Test-2" button, wait for 1 minute, copy and save the console output.

Step 4: click the "Test-3" button, wait for 1 minute, copy and save the console output.

Step 5: provide the saved logs to whoever is requesting.
